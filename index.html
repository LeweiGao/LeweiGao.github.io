I am a GIS analyst and urban planner in training, currently completing a Master of Urban Innovation at the University of Toronto. My background sits at the intersection of spatial analysis, urban policy, and public-sector decision-making.

My interests lie at the intersection of urban design and geospatial analysis — focusing on how humans interact with urban environments and using spatial data and visualization to uncover meaningful insights into the dynamics of cities.

I aim to contribute to sustainable urban development and the creation of vibrant, inclusive public spaces, where data-driven solutions shape the future of urban landscapes.
